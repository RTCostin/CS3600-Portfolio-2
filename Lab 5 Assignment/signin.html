Sign In

This page allows existing users to enter their account information in a clean and simple interface. It demonstrates the use of forms and Bootstrap styling for layout and usability.

A sign-in page is a common feature in modern websites because it gives users access to personalized content and secure sections of a site. Building this page shows knowledge of forms, labels, inputs, and buttons.

Even though this example does not connect to a database, it still serves as a professional front-end design example that meets the assignment requirements.
