About Me

A little more about who I am and what I enjoy doing.

My Background

Hello! My name is Ryan Costin, and I am a passionate programmer and a dedicated student. I enjoy learning about technology, solving problems, and building projects that strengthen my coding skills and creativity.

I am currently enrolled at the University of Idaho pursuing my bachelor's degree, and I plan to continue my education to further enhance my skills and knowledge. My academic experience has helped me develop a strong foundation in programming, software development, and analytical thinking.

My interest in programming started with curiosity about how systems work and how developers create useful tools and applications. Since then, I have continued improving my skills through coursework, projects, and personal practice.

My Interests

Outside of programming, I enjoy spending time outdoors, exercising, and being active. Hiking is one of my favorite activities, especially when I can turn it into a fun competition with my wife.

I also enjoy fantasy movies and stories, especially The Lord of the Rings franchise. Creative storytelling and immersive worlds are a big part of what inspires my imagination.

In addition to technology and entertainment, I also appreciate the simple things in life, including a really good turkey sandwich. These interests help keep my life balanced while I continue growing as a student and developer.
